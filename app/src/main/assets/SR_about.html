The Stereo Rendering sample shows how to enable a stereo view for digital eyewear.

Key Functionality

  • Enable stereo for video see-through digital eyewear that is powered by a mobile device
  • Move between full screen and stereo on a mobile device
  • Render in stereo on optical see-through digital eyewear

Targets

Stones

Instructions

  • Choose full screen or stereo from the About page (mobile device only)
  • Look at the stones target to see a teapot rendered in stereo
